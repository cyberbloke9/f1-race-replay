    4. **test_mismatch_penalty_reduces_health_faster** — Create two models with same profile:

       Feed same lap times to both for 10 laps. Model B should have lower health than Model A.

    5. **test_model_handles_single_lap** — Feed just 1 lap. Should not crash. Health should be valid.

    6. **test_model_handles_many_laps** — Feed 60 laps (long stint). Should not crash, health should be >= 0.
